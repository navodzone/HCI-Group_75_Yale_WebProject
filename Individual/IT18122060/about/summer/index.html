Yale Norfolk School of Art

Established in 1948, the Yale Norfolk School of Art is an intensive six-week undergraduate summer residency program for 26 rising seniors.

Yale Summer Session

Undergraduate-level courses are offered at the Yale School of Art through Yale Summer Session.

The Artist Collective Summer School

Launched in 2019, the Artist Collective Summer School is a two-week program for current MFA students hosted in coordination with the Paul Mellon Centre, the Yale Art History Department and the Yale Center for British Art
